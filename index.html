About Me!

My name is Ariana Jacpasad, I am a game developer and game designer who is from South Africa. I attend University of Witwatersrand. Here you can see my writings, the art made for different games, the games I have developed as well small things I call my test subjects.

I have been interested in video games since a young age and naturally became interested in the development and design process. I always wondered how I would be able to provide an experience and my ideas in an interactive way that was able to be enjoyed by others.
