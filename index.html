About Me!

My name is Ariana Jacpasad, I am a game developer and game designer who is from South Africa. I attend the University of Witwatersrand. Here you can see my writings, the art made for different games, the games I have developed as well as small things I call my 'test subjects. I have been interested in video games since a younger age and naturally became interested in the development and design process. I have always been a creative person who was desperate to share it with the world and games are a great way to achieve this while allowing others to interact with my creations.
